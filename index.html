About Me

I am a research Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at SRM University. Prior to this, I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore), India, where my thesis focused on the "Translation generated oblique dual frames by actions of locally compact groups".

Research Interests & Skills

  • Research Interests: Harmonic analysis; Frame theory; Locally compact group; Fourier analysis on non-abelian groups; Nilpotent Lie groups; Heisenberg group; Hardy Space, etc.
